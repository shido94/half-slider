About Us

Project4Jungle is a service provider for creating natural, wild, maintenance free, native forests. At Project4Jungle, we work passionately on and off-site to provide the best solutions at the lowest possible cost. Started in September 2018, Project4Jungle is a social enterprise on a mission to bring back our native forests that have been lost. We do it by creating them.
